Clinical Attachment Programme for IMGs

Frequently Asked Questions

A Clinical Attachment is an observational placement in a GP surgery where international medical graduates (IMGs) can gain insight into how the NHS functions. Please note, this role is strictly observational and does not involve hands-on clinical work.

The programme is specifically designed for International Medical Graduates (IMGs) who want to gain first-hand exposure to the UK healthcare system. Applicants should hold a recognised medical qualification.

We offer two options: a 6-week programme (£600) and a 4-week programme (£500).

Although accommodation is not provided, assistance can be offered in finding local accommodation if required.

A reference is not automatically provided to all participants. References are reserved for doctors who demonstrate excellence and consistently show competencies in line with GMC Good Medical Practice throughout their clinical attachment.

While we are happy to accept full payment on your first day of starting the programme, this does not guarantee your placement as allocation is on a first-come, first-served basis. A £100 deposit is required to confirm your place.
