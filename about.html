Dr Mahdi has a particular passion for neurodiversity, advocating for inclusive healthcare and raising awareness through education. He is committed to supporting learners at all levels, offering mentorship, practical skills training, and guidance to help them thrive in both academic and clinical settings.

Hoyland Medical Practice has a strong ethos of education, mentorship, and continuous learning, offering a supportive environment for both undergraduate students and postgraduate doctors in specialty training. We believe that hands-on experience with real patients, under the close supervision of practicing clinicians, is the most effective way to grow in confidence, improve clinical skills, and become the best doctor you can be.
