Overview

The Clinical Attachment Programme provides International Medical Graduates (IMGs) with an opportunity to observe and participate in clinical practice within an NHS setting under supervision. It helps participants gain familiarity with NHS systems, clinical communication, and multidisciplinary collaboration.

Programme Structure

The programme consists of clinical shadowing, structured teaching sessions, and supervised patient interactions. Participants will be assigned to a supervising GP who will guide them throughout the attachment.

Additional Features

  • Observation of GP consultations and multidisciplinary meetings.
  • Exposure to primary care systems and electronic medical records.
  • Guidance on GMC registration and NHS recruitment pathways.
  • Feedback and support from experienced clinicians.

Who Should Apply?

This programme is ideal for International Medical Graduates seeking UK clinical experience, orientation to NHS culture, and preparation for GMC registration or UK employment.

Documents Required

  • Passport copy
  • Proof of medical qualification
  • Updated CV
  • Occupational health clearance (if available)
