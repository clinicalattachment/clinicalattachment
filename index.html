Clinical Attachment Placements

This Clinical Attachment Programme is specifically designed for International Medical Graduates (IMGs) seeking firsthand exposure to the UK healthcare system. It offers intensive, structured, and fully supervised clinical experience within an NHS GP practice. Participants will benefit from 1-1 mentorship by GP Partners, PLAB 2 OSCE Examiners, and Educational Supervisors, helping them develop core clinical competencies, professional confidence, and familiarity with NHS protocols.

Cohorts & Availability

6 week programmes are available upon request and subject to availability.

We offer clinical attachment placements in set cohorts throughout the year. Places are subject to availability and allocated on a first come, first served basis.

Cost: 6-week programme for £600   |   4-week programme for £500 (deposit: £100.00). Both payments are non-refundable.

What Clinical Attachments Does Hoyland Medical Practice Offer?

We offer clinical attachment placements in General Practice settings. Please note that all placements are observational only, and patient safety and confidentiality remain the priority.

Clinical Attachment Entry Criteria

Applicants must meet the below criteria and be able to show evidence for each.

  • Applicants must be an international medical graduate (IMG).
  • Non-EU Doctors – Applicants must have PLAB 1 and PLAB 2 or be working towards PLAB 2.
  • Applicants must have completed IELTS at 7.5 or above (OET also accepted).
  • Applicants must provide an Overseas Police Certificate.
  • Applicants must have graduated no more than 5 years ago.
  • Applicants are only permitted one placement at Hoyland Medical Practice and must not have already undertaken one here previously.

How Do I Apply?

Follow these steps to apply:

  1. Download and complete the Clinical Attachment Application Form (incomplete forms will be declined).
  2. Return your completed form, CV, and supporting evidence to info@clinicalattachment.com.
  3. Your request will be reviewed and you will be contacted with the outcome.
  4. If successful, you will be asked to pay the £100 deposit by the stated deadline and send evidence of payment.
  5. Complete Occupational Health requirements by the deadline (required to progress).
  6. Pay the final balance at least 2 weeks before the start date.
  7. You will be sent a placement agreement and induction information.

Frequently Asked Questions

What will my placement involve?

It will depend on the area of General Practice you observe. Please note the clinical attachment is observational only.

I’ve missed the start date; can I still apply?

No — if the cohort has already started you cannot apply, but you can apply for the next one.
