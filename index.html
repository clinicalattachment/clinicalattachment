Overview

Hoyland Medical Practice offers a Clinical Attachment Programme specifically designed for International Medical Graduates (IMGs) seeking firsthand exposure to the UK healthcare system. The programme provides intensive, structured, and fully supervised clinical experience within an NHS GP practice. Participants receive one-to-one mentorship from GP Partners, PLAB 2 OSCE Examiners, and Educational Supervisors, supporting the development of core clinical competencies, professional confidence, and familiarity with NHS protocols.

Programme Fee

We offer two Clinical Attachment Programmes:

  • A) 6-week programme – £600
  • B) 4-week programme – £500

To secure your placement, a £100 deposit is required, with the remaining balance payable according to your chosen programme. Please note that both the deposit and final payment are non-refundable.

While we are happy to accept full payment on your first day of starting the programme, this does not guarantee your placement, as allocations are made on a first-come, first-served basis.
