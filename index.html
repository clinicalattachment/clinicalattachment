Overview

Hoyland Medical Practice offers a Clinical Attachment Programme specifically designed for International Medical Graduates (IMGs) seeking firsthand exposure to the UK healthcare system. The programme provides intensive, structured, and fully supervised clinical experience within an NHS GP practice. Participants receive one-to-one mentorship from GP Partners, PLAB 2 OSCE Examiners, and Educational Supervisors, supporting the development of core clinical competencies, professional confidence, and familiarity with NHS protocols.

Programme Structure

  • Supervised hands-on experience in history-taking and clinical examinations
  • Exposure to real NHS patient consultations and case discussions
  • Training in documentation, prescribing, referrals, and NHS consultation models
  • Structured debriefs following patient consultations
  • Creation of a personalised learning plan targeting individual areas for improvement
  • Participation in quality improvement (QI) projects and audits for portfolio development
  • Teaching on UK guidelines, medical ethics, and effective communication
  • Opportunities to accompany clinicians on home visits and nursing home ward rounds
  • Observation of multidisciplinary teams, including Advanced Nurse Practitioners, clinical pharmacists, and physiotherapists
  • Observation of minor surgery sessions, joint injections, implant insertions, and more

Additional Features

  • Regular feedback and performance reviews
  • Targeted support to address clinical and communication gaps
  • Career guidance, including GP training insights, CV building, interview preparation, and teaching sessions with registrars and trainers
  • Accommodation assistance (if required)

Who Should Apply?

  • IMGs preparing for GMC registration or recently registered
  • IMGs unfamiliar with NHS systems and clinical expectations
  • Doctors seeking taster weeks in General Practice
  • Those wanting to gain practical experience in UK primary care

Documents Required

  • Passport
  • Visa (if Non-EU). We are unable to provide visa sponsorship letters
  • Proof of qualification and professional registration (and translation)
  • DBS / CRB / Criminal Records check from home country (and translation)
  • An up-to-date CV
  • GMC Registration Status (if applicable)
  • A brief summary of your learning aims from the programme

Programme Fee

  • 6-week programme – £600
  • 4-week programme – £500

To secure your placement, a £100 deposit is required, with the remaining balance payable according to your chosen programme.

Both the deposit and final payment are non-refundable. Full payment on your first day does not guarantee placement; allocations are first-come, first-served.

Application Process for Clinical Attachment

Below are the steps of the application process:

Step 1: Complete the Application Form

  • Fill in the application form below (all fields are mandatory).
  • Incomplete forms may delay processing.
  • Each cohort has a maximum of 2 placements; allocation is strictly first-come, first-served.

Step 2: Submit Your Documents

  • Email required documents to syicb-barnsley.hoyland@nhs.net
  • Refer to Documents Required section for the full list.
  • We will review and verify your documents and notify you once approved.

Step 3: Secure Your Placement with Payment

  • £100 deposit secures your attachment dates.
  • Final balance can be paid immediately or on the first day (depends on chosen programme).
  • Place is only guaranteed after deposit is received.

Step 4: Optional Informal Visit

  • You may arrange a 45-minute tour of The Hoyland Centre.
  • Meet members of our team, including doctors.
  • Deposit payment may also be made on the same day.

Step 5: Induction & Start of Placement

  • Attend induction at the GP practice on the first day.
  • Your personalised timetable will be sent one week before placement.

Cancellations

  • If unable to attend or wish to withdraw, notify via syicb-barnsley.hoyland@nhs.net.
  • All fees paid are non-refundable.
